ABOUTUS 

Welcome to CyberHive! We are a passionate community of students, professionals, and enthusiasts dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of cybersecurity. Our mission is to build a hub for learning, collaboration, and innovation in the fast-evolving field of digital security. From network defense and ethical hacking to cryptography and threat analysis, we dive deep into the skills and knowledge needed to safeguard today’s digital landscape.

One of our cornerstone initiatives is Cyber Siksha Sessions (CSL), held every Saturday. These sessions cover a structured, interactive environment where we dive into key topics in cybersecurity, work through hands-on labs, and participate in activities designed to sharpen our skills. From ethical hacking and network defense to digital forensics and cryptography, each session is crafted to build both foundational and advanced knowledge. Whether you're a beginner just starting out or a seasoned pro looking to hone your skills, our club provides a supportive environment for all levels.

This year, we are thrilled to take part in Gyanotsav 2025, our college’s annual event and tech fest, which is a celebration of innovation, technology, and creativity. Gyanotsav hosts a variety of events, including technical competitions, workshops, and seminars. As a key participant in the fest, Cyber Hive has been given the opportunity to organize a lineup of tech-centered activities, all crafted to challenge and inspire. Our events are designed to bring together budding cybersecurity enthusiasts, programmers, and techies, o ering them a platform to test their skills, collaborate, and explore emerging trends.

The tech fest within Gyanotsav is where CyberHive truly shines. We’re hosting CRACK-IN 1.0 CTF which is a capture-the-flag competition, giving students a chance to immerse themselves in real-world cyber scenarios. By participating in this event, members not only enhance their technical abilities but also contribute to the vibrant campus culture that makes Gyan Ganga a hub of innovation and technology.

CyberHive’s role in Gyan Utsav exemplifies our commitment to fostering a security conscious campus and cultivating the next generation of cyber experts. Join us and become a part of a dynamic community where learning meets action, and enthusiasm for cybersecurity fuels a future of possibilities.

A CTF is an interactive cybersecurity competition where participants solve challenges that simulate real-world cyber scenarios. It’s a hands-on experience in which players decode hidden clues, test vulnerabilities, and protect digital assets to “capture” virtual flags. Each challenge is a new test of skill and creativity—whether you’re into cryptography, reverse engineering, or network analysis, there’s something in this competition for you.

Our CRACK-IN 1.0 CTF is open to all students from any technical field and isn’t limited to our college; any tech enthusiast from across the country is welcome to join in. This is your chance to put your skills to the test, learn from like-minded peers, and experience the thrill of solving real-world problems in a supportive environment. At Cyber Hive, we believe in learning by doing, and this CTF is a chance to dive deep into cybersecurity while having fun, connecting with others, and challenging yourself.

If you’re excited about stepping into the world of cybersecurity, or if you’re just curious about what it’s all about, we invite you to join us. Together, let’s explore the challenges of cybersecurity, one flag at a time.

The SkyTech Innovations was on the verge of unveiling its cutting-edge drone for agricultural surveillance. However, a group of cybercriminals, led by the notorious Phantom, stole the blueprints. You’ve intercepted a suspicious executable, droneControl.exe, from Phantom’s laptop. Reverse-engineer the program to find the password that unlocks the blueprint file.

The Cursed Artifact80 points
Solvers: 76  Difficulty:

In Varanasi, Dr. Nitin Awasthi, an archaeologist, discovered an ancient artifact rumored to unlock the secrets of an ancient Indian tomb. The artifact, a peculiar image called artifact.png is believed to contain the key to entering the tomb. Your task is to use steganography to extract a Base64 string from the image and then decrypt it using AES-256 with an IV hidden in the image’s metadata. Combine the decrypted data to form the tomb’s passphrase and unlock the artifact's secret.

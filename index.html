OVERVIEW 

The CyberHive Club is proud to host Crack-In 1.0, a national-level Capture the Flag (CTF) competition, as part of our ongoing mission to empower the next generation of cybersecurity enthusiasts. Organized under the banner of Gyan Ganga Institute of Technology & Science, this event offers a thrilling platform for participants to showcase their skills, tackle challenging problems, and learn from peers in the cybersecurity community.

Key Highlights of Crack-In 1.0


• Event Type: Jeopardy-style CTF with a diverse range of categories, including Web Exploitation, Cryptography, Reverse Engineering, Digital Forensics, and Binary Exploitation.

• Level: Designed for all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals.

• Collaborative Learning: Encourages teamwork, problem-solving, and the application of ethical hacking techniques.

• Prizes and Recognition: Participants compete for exciting rewards, certificates, and the chance to gain recognition among top cybersecurity talent.
